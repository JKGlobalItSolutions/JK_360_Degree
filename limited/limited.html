Overview of LLP Registration Online in India

LLP Registration is a trendy and well-known business structure in the world. LLP and pvt ltd companies are registered with the Ministry of Business Affairs under the Central Government. Get your LLP registered & process registration as an LLP with Kanakkupillai.

One of the most popular types of businesses for entrepreneurs is the limited liability partnership or LLP. LLPs are the most straightforward business organization due to the benefit of restricted liability. Limited Liability Partnership registration in India gives complete freedom to partners to form a partnership business structure in which the liability of each member or partner is limited to the sum of money they lend into the business. To clarify, if the partnership fails in this business structure, creditors or investors cannot demand the partners personal assets, property, or income.



Advantages of Limited Liability Partnership Registration

Limited liability

The extent of each partner's liability in an LLP is determined by the contribution they made to the business. This is the most crucial aspect of an LLP since it protects all of the members' personal assets and eliminates the need to cover any losses or debt incurred by the company. Furthermore, innocent LLP or Limited Liability Partnership partners are not required to pay for the wrongdoings of another partner in the company.

Separate legal entity

The LLP is regarded as a distinct legal entity. This indicates that the company is liable for debts and possesses assets in its name. Additionally, neither partner is accountable or responsible for another partner's negligence or improper behaviour.

Flexible agreement

Regarding the rules and regulations or rights and duties, the partners of the LLP registration firm are free to prepare and form the agreement as they need.


No manager/owner distinction

Partners in a limited liability partnership company run and own the company. Although its directors and stockholders may not be the same, an llp differs from a private limited company. Because of this, VCs do not fund or invest in the Limited Liability Partnership company.


Less compliance needed

Compared to a private limited company, a limited liability partnership is a simpler and more affordable business form to manage because it only requires three annual compliances. However, a private limited corporation must maintain several observations and do regular audits in order to comply with the provision.


Easy to wind-up

In contrast to a private limited company, a limited liability partnership is not only simpler to establish but also simpler to dissolve. The winding-up procedure typically takes two to three months to complete, whereas closing a private limited company may take longer than a year.

Registration procedure of Limited Liability Partnership Firm

Documents & Information For LLP Registration

The process of LLP registration starts with the arrangement of all required documents of the partners, designated partners, registered business addresses, and the LLP deed. You must provide all these documents to us before we begin the process of LLP registration for you. Make sure the records are accurate and up to date. Fill out the online questionnaire and furnish the required details.

Selection of Name for LLP

The name of an LLP must not be identical to the name of an existing company or LLP or an applied or registered trademark. You can check the availability of the suggested name on the database of LLP and companies or the database of registered and applied trademarks, available on the websites of MCA and IP India, respectively. Follow the guidelines to select a valid name for your LLP.

Obtaining DSC of the Applicant

The process to register an LLP is completely online. The application form for registration of LLPs must be filled out online, and the required supporting documents must be uploaded along with it, which must be signed using a digital signature certificate of the applicant's designated partner. You can also subscribe to our services to obtain a DSC.

Name Approval for LLP Registration

After you have selected a valid name for your LLP, you must reserve it using the RUN application available on the MCA website. You can propose two names per application and get two chances of resubmission if the first two names get invalidated.

5
Fill out and submit the FiLLiP form

FiLLiP form is the form filled out and accepted by the Registrar of Companies for incorporating an LLP in India. The form is required to be signed by anyone designated partner of the LLP using a DSC and be submitted with all necessary supporting documents uploaded with it. Its application fee depends on the authorised capital of the LLP.

Issue of the Certificate of Incorporation of LLP

After the FiLLiP form is submitted to the ROC, the ROC inspects and verifies it thoroughly. Only after he is satisfied with the details furnished in the application and the certificate attached with it, he shall register the LLP and issue a Certificate of Registration to the LLP. Along with the Certificate, the ROC also allots an Identification number to the registered LLP.

STEP 7 Drafting of LLP Agreement and Filing to ROC

Post-registration of the LLP, the partners must enter into a valid LLP deed and draft the same on the stamp paper. The deed defines the mutual rights and duties of the partners. It should be signed by all partners in the presence of a notary, who shall stamp the deed. The deed is then filed with ROC in Form -3 within 30 days from the date on which the document of Registration was issued.

PAN Card & TAN Number for the LLP

The PAN and TAN of the LLP can be applied in the forms 49 A and 49 B, as prescribed under the Income Tax Act. Both PAN and TAN are allotted by the Income Tax Department of India. While PAN is necessary for paying income tax, TAN is required to gain entitlement to deduct and collect tax at source.

FAQ's on LLP Registration

What is DSC?

The DSC is nothing but a Digital Signature Certificate issued by certifying authorities (TCS and n-Code) using that certificate you can sign electronic documents. As all documents need a DSC to start up a LLP in India.

What is the Director Identification Number or DIN?

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs issues applicants on whose name the application is produced a Director Identification Number, a special number that enables the person to serve as a Director in the Company.

What is meant by Contribution in LLP?

The Contribution in LLP means the share of the partners in the form of money, promissory notes, tangible or intangible property, agreements, movable or immovable assets to the Company

How many days are required to complete the registering process of a limited liability partnership firm in India?

Generally, it takes around 10 working days for the registration process of a limited liability partnership firm in India.

How many partners are required to register LLP?

As per the LLP Act, 2008, a minimum of two partners is needed to register an LLP.

Who can become a partner in a limited liability partnership firm?

Any individuals, company or foreign national can become a partner in an LLP

What are the rules to be considered while selecting the name of a Limited liability Company in India?

The name of a Limited Liability Company should be unique and applicable to the service which your LLP is providing. The selected name must not be generic and similar to any other existing name

Who prefers to register LLPs?

An LLP is an advancement over a partnership firm with the features of the corporate form of business, and the LLPs are most suitable for professionals like CAs, CS, Advocates, Engineers, Doctors and small businesses with low turnover. Kindly note that there is no restriction in the law concerning the suitability of the LLP vis a vis any form of business.
